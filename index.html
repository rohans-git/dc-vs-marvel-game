DC vs Marvel: Who Owns the Universe?

Witness the ultimate clash of two superhero icons as Batman takes on Captain America in a battle for the ages, pitting brains against brawn and Gotham against New York. Who will emerge victorious in this epic showdown?

Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow to navigate and use SPACE to shoot DESTROY the sheilds! Use P to pause the game.

Avoid contact with the shield as it will reduce your power, and eventually lose if your immunity dies.

When you level up, you might see power-ups appearing. Capture them to boost your gameplay experience. You win the game when you reach 500 points.
